# OpenChrome v1.10.3 (staging)

Release date: TBD

## Summary

v1.10.3 is a small **patch release** that bounds the per-instance cookie
caches on `CDPClient` to cap long-running-instance heap growth.

## What changed

- `src/cdp/client.ts`: the `cookieSourceCache` and `cookieDataCache` are
  now bounded (`COOKIE_SOURCE_CACHE_MAX = 64`, `COOKIE_DATA_CACHE_MAX = 16`).
  FIFO eviction runs on write when at cap, and stale entries are deleted
  on read-miss past `COOKIE_CACHE_TTL`. No public API, TTL, or
  `onTargetDestroyed` semantics change. See
  [issue #647](https://github.com/shaun0927/openchrome/issues/647).
